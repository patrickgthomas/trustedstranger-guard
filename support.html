Frequently Asked Questions

When will the app be available?
Gide – Guard is currently being prepared for launch on the App Store.
What does Guard do?
Guard helps users analyze suspicious emails, text messages, links, websites, and other communications for potential scams, phishing attempts, and fraud risks.
Do I need an account?
No. The core Guard experience is designed to be simple and accessible without creating an account.
Can Guard tell me with certainty whether something is a scam?
No. Guard provides risk assessments and educational information to help users identify red flags and make informed decisions. Users should always exercise caution when sharing personal information or sending money.
What types of scams can Guard help identify?
Guard can help identify common phishing emails, fraudulent text messages, fake websites, impersonation scams, marketplace scams, investment scams, and other suspicious communications.
Is my information shared?
We do not sell your personal information. Information submitted for analysis may be processed by trusted third-party services as described in our Privacy Policy.
