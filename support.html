Frequently Asked Questions

When will the app be available?
Trusted Stranger – Guard is currently being prepared for launch on the App Store.
Do I need an account?
No. The core Guard experience is designed to be simple and accessible without creating an account.
Can I save favorite verses and prayers?
Yes. Favorite verses, prayers, and reflections can be saved for quick access later.
Can I receive daily reminders?
Yes. You may enable daily notifications to receive scripture and encouragement reminders.
Is my information shared?
No. Trusted Stranger – Guard does not sell your personal information.
