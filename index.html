FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Is Gide – Guard available now?
Yes. Gide – Guard is available on the App Store as a simple tool to help users recognize suspicious messages and scam warning signs.
What types of scams can Guard help identify?
Guard is designed to help identify phishing emails, fraudulent text messages, fake websites, impersonation scams, online marketplace scams, suspicious links, and other risky communications.
Does Guard guarantee that a message is safe?
No. Gide – Guard provides guidance and risk awareness to help you make a more informed decision. No app can guarantee that every message, website, person, or offer is safe.
Does Guard replace professional cybersecurity help?
No. Gide – Guard is an educational and personal-use tool. It is not a substitute for professional cybersecurity, legal, financial, or fraud investigation services.
