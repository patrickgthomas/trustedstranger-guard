Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Introduction

Trusted Stranger – Guard is designed to provide daily meditation, breathing exercises, reflection prompts, affirmations, and color noise tools. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our app or website.

Information We Collect

Trusted Stranger – Guard is designed to collect as little personal information as possible. The app does not require users to create an account for the core experience.

Depending on the features used, the app may store information such as:

  • Favorite meditations, affirmations, or reflection prompts
  • Notification preferences
  • Theme and text size preferences
  • Color noise settings and timer preferences
  • Basic app settings

This information is generally stored locally on your device and is used only to provide app functionality.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not intentionally collect:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your precise location
  • Your contacts
  • Financial information
  • Health information
  • Government identification numbers

How We Use Information

Information stored by the app is used to provide features such as saved favorites, daily reminders, display preferences, color noise settings, and app customization.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, Trusted Stranger – Guard may send daily meditation, affirmation, or reflection reminders. You can turn notifications on or off through the app settings or your device settings.

Color Noise Generator

Trusted Stranger – Guard includes locally generated color noise features, such as white, pink, brown, blue, purple, gray, and green noise. These sounds are generated and played on your device and do not require internet connectivity.

Data Storage

Most app preferences, favorites, and settings are stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, locally stored information may also be deleted.

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services for app distribution, hosting, diagnostics, or basic performance monitoring. These services may process limited technical information according to their own privacy policies.

No Sale of Personal Information

We do not sell your personal information.

Health Disclaimer

Trusted Stranger – Guard is intended for general wellness, mindfulness, and relaxation purposes only. The app does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or mental health services.

Children’s Privacy

Trusted Stranger – Guard is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Choices

You may disable notifications, delete saved favorites, adjust app preferences, modify color noise settings, or uninstall the app at any time.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date.
